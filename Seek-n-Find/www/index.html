Picture Quest


Picture Quest is a scavenger hunt game. The aim of the game is to search around taking pictures of the items on the list. Picture Quest is a great excuse to go outside and explore your neighbourhood, what are you waiting for!


How to play:
1. Click on "Start"
2. Select how many items you want to search
3. When you find an item on the list click on the camera icon and snap a picture!

Hint: You may interpret the items on the list as you please.
